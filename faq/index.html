Why DataPrePrep?

DataPrePrep is data pre processing package which is there to decrease your time while doing analysis stuff. let there be some missing values in your data set or some outliers.

you can easily process them with just one line of code. Earlier your approach would be to think and apply some logic to process these stuffs. But with using our package you can easily do all these thereby improving your efficiency and increasing your performance by a factor of 2.
